Evolution of pulsar magnetic fields

Together with Ashley Bransgrove and Andrei Beloborodov, I have developed a numerical framework to study the evolution of neutron-star magnetic field. Our formalism incorporated many important physial effects: the interaction between the crust and the hall, the Hall-wave dynamics, and elastic response of the crust.

Magnetic field evolution of neutron stars - I. Basic formalism, numerical techniques and first results.
