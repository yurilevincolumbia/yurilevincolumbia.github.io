Neutron star seismology

Vibrational modes of the Eatch and the Sun are routinely measured and provide a wealth of information about their internal structure. Observing a vibrational mode of a neutron star woud provide a wealth of information about their internal structure, and this is one of the holy grails of neutron star astrophysics. I have worked on the theory of two types of vibrational modes: the r-modes of rapidly rotating neutron stars, and torsional magneto-elastic modes of magnetars.
